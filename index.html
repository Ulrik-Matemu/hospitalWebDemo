Vision

Our vision at Siha Polyclinic Limited is encapsulated in our motto "We Care, God Cure". This guiding principle points to our aspiration to create a healthier community in Tanga and the surrounding area. We envision a future where every resident has access to comprehensive, advanced, and compassionate healthcare services right at their doorstep. We Strive to be a beacon of hope and health, not just in Tanga, but in the broader region, setting new standards in healthcare delivery and patient satisfaction.

Mission

Our mission guides our day-to-day operations and strategic decisions. We aim to provide an extensive range of high-quality healthcare services that meet the diverse needs of our community. Our commitment is to ensure that these services are not only excellent but also accessible and affordable for everyone . As we progress on our journey, our mission is to continuously expand and improve our facilities and services.The ultimate goal is to evolve Siha Polyclinic Limited into a fully-fledged hospital, a centre of excellence for healthcare, trusted and respected by the community we serve.
