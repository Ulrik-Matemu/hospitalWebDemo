Who We Are

Siha Polyclinic & Diagnostic Centre (SPDC) is a new state-of-art medical centre, registered with PHAB and it started operating on October 2019, ever since it has been growing enormously. SPDC is located at TANGA REGION-Central ward, Market Street-Near NBC and AMANA bank. SPDC was the FIRST centre equipped with MRI, CT-Scan, ECHO and EEG Machine. We are committed to providing superior quality health care services to address the day-to-day health care needs for our community who would otherwise travel to other bigger cities for such investigations. The main objective of Siha Polyclinic is to offer the best possible health solutions with utmost care to all at affordable prices Our centre is fully equipped with modern diagnostic Equipments. We are a completely digital department allowing us to provide the highest level of service, with the latest technology available.

Vision

At Siha Polyclinic Limited, our motto, "We Care, God Cure," reflects our vision of fostering a healthier Tanga and surrounding communities. We aim to ensure accessible, advanced, and compassionate healthcare for all, becoming a beacon of hope and setting new standards in regional healthcare excellence.

Mission

Our mission drives our daily operations and strategic goals. We strive to deliver high-quality, accessible, and affordable healthcare to meet our community's diverse needs. As we grow, we aim to enhance our services and facilities, ultimately transforming Siha Polyclinic Limited into a trusted and respected hospital and a center of excellence in healthcare.
