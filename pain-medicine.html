Transform Your Life with Effective Spine & Pain Management

Chronic pain like persistent back pain, joint discomfort, or severe headaches, can take a significant toll on your daily activities and emotional well-being. Whatever the root cause like a sports or accidental injury, sickness, or underlying health issues, you don’t need to endure pain for the rest of your life.

Siha Polyclinic Pain Medicine Department focuses on

  • Targeting the source of your pain
  • Implementing personalised treatment plans like minimally invasive procedures
  • Prolonged or permanent solution to pain compared to painkillers which can only provide temporary relief and can lead to dependency
  • Improving overall function and quality of life, enabling you to return to your daily activities more effectively

At Siha Polyclinic, we understand the struggle and are dedicated to providing compassionate, comprehensive care to help you manage and alleviate your pain.

Comprehensive Spine & Pain Management Services

Interventional Pain Management

Using minimally invasive techniques like nerve blocks and spinal injections to directly target the source of pain, reducing inflammation and interrupting pain signals.

Joint Pain Treatment

Addressing conditions such as osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and sports injuries through methods like viscosupplementation, steroid injections, and physical therapy to restore joint function and relieve pain.

Cancer Pain Management

Providing relief for pain associated with cancer and its treatment, including medication management, nerve blocks, and advanced interventions like intrathecal pumps to improve patient comfort and quality of life.

Back Pain Treatment

Treating conditions like herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and sciatica with options such as epidural steroid injections, radiofrequency ablation, and physical therapy to reduce pain and improve mobility.

Headache Management

Specialized treatments for migraines, tension headaches, and cluster headaches, including nerve blocks, Botox injections, and lifestyle modifications to reduce frequency and severity.

Neuromodulation

Employing techniques such as spinal cord stimulation and peripheral nerve stimulation to modulate pain signals and provide relief for chronic pain conditions unresponsive to other treatments.

Intrathecal Pump Implantation

Using implanted pumps to deliver pain medication directly to the spinal fluid, providing targeted relief for severe chronic pain conditions with fewer side effects than oral medications.

Knee and Neck Pain Treatment

Utilizing advanced interventions like radiofrequency ablation and regenerative medicine techniques to address pain from conditions such as osteoarthritis and cervical spondylosis, enhancing function and reducing discomfort.

Trigeminal Neuralgia Treatment

Offering specialized care for trigeminal neuralgia, including microvascular decompression, glycerol injections, and radiofrequency rhizotomy to manage severe facial pain.

Arthritis and Pain Management

Comprehensive management of arthritis pain through a combination of pharmacologic treatments, physical therapy, and interventional procedures to improve joint function and reduce pain.

Spinal Diseases and Slip Disc Treatment

Expert diagnosis and treatment of conditions like degenerative disc disease, spondylolisthesis, and slipped discs using minimally invasive surgeries and non-surgical interventions to alleviate pain and improve spinal health.

Spinal Cord Stimulation Devices and Neurolysis

Implementing spinal cord stimulators and neurolysis techniques to disrupt pain pathways and provide long-term relief for chronic pain sufferers.
