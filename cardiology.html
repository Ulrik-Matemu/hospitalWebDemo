WE CARE, GOD CURES

Cardiovascular disease (CVD) strikes Indians a decade earlier in their lives when compared to western nations, and over 28% of Indians die from CVD-related causes.

With expert physicians, state-of-the-art diagnostic, treatment and post-surgical recovery care, the Siha Polyclinic Department of Cardiology aims to make a valuable contribution to reduce the burden of CVD.

The Siha Polyclinic Department of Cardiology offers best-in-class holistic, non-invasive cardiac care and helps patients effectively and safely manage their cardiac conditions.

Request for Cardiology Service

Department of Cardiology

Adult Cardiology

Diagnosis and treatment services for heart-related conditions in adults, including congenital heart defects, coronary artery disease, valvular heart disease and heart failure, among many others.

Paediatric Cardiology

Our expert paediatric cardiologists are highly experienced in evaluating, diagnosing and treating both congenital and acquired heart and vascular problems in children and adolescents aged 18 and under.

Foetal Echocardiography

Using state-of-the-art technology, we offer an in-depth evaluation of the structure, condition and function of your developing baby’s heart while in the womb.

Cardiac Profile – Health Check Package

The most beautiful rhythm is the steady beat of a healthy heart. Get a cardiac profile to find out how to keep your heart happy and improve your quality of life.

Exercise Related Cardiac Screening

Start your exercise journey with peace of mind by ensuring your heart is healthy enough to support your fitness goals. Understand your current status, uncover ‘silent’ heart-related problems and exercise safely with a pre-gym cardiac screening.

Home Healthcare - Cardiology Service

Recovering from a cardiac ailment or surgery? Enjoy a stress-free recovery process with Siha Polyclinic Home Healthcare Cardiology Service. With cardiac monitoring, education and personal care tailored to your needs, we help you manage your recovery in the comfort of your own home.

Cardiovascular Physical Therapy

Blending health education, risk reduction strategies, physical activity and stress management, Siha Polyclinic offers cardiac rehabilitation services for a range of heart-related issues, including CABG, DVR, MVR, CHD and cardiopulmonary ailments.

Corporate Services (Cardiology)

Workplace stress and poor health habits can take a toll on employee health, productivity and business. Support your employees with Siha Polyclinic corporate cardiology services. Whether onsite or remote using telehealth technology, we offer seamless support and advanced cardiology care, education and policy guidance.

Diagnostic Services / Tests at Siha Polyclinic

Department of Cardiology at Siha Polyclinic is equipped with the following latest machines and tests which help in accurate diagnosis and management of heart related problems:

Facilities Available for Cardiology Tests:

  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)

  • Echocardiogram- Adult, Paediatric and Fetal

  • Cardiac CT scan

  • Holter Monitoring (CDL technology)

  • Exercise stress test or Treadmill test

  • Chest X-ray

  • Blood tests

  • Dietary consultation
