About Us

Our Phygital Presence

Siha Polyclinic Healthcare is a Tanga-based NABH-NABL accredited multispeciality healthcare chain. Founded in 2019, our company is guided by the core principles of delivering the best health outcomes, at optimal cost, with utmost patient satisfaction.

Doctors from over 40 disciplines practice at our facilities, complemented by a global presence through a large pool of teleconsultants. For patients unable to visit our centres, we bring healthcare to their doorstep. Our centres are equipped with well-stocked pharmacies, state-of-the-art radiology, and diagnostic labs. We also partner with corporates, institutions, sports federations, and social organisations to deliver tailored healthcare services.

We are your neighbourhood healthcare partner, delivering world-class care with compassion and innovation. Siha Polyclinic remains, phygitally yours.

Vision

At Siha Polyclinic Limited, our motto, "We Care, God Cure," reflects our vision of fostering a healthier Tanga and surrounding communities. We aim to ensure accessible, advanced, and compassionate healthcare for all, becoming a beacon of hope and setting new standards in regional healthcare excellence.

Mission

Our mission drives our daily operations and strategic goals. We strive to deliver high-quality, accessible, and affordable healthcare to meet our community's diverse needs. As we grow, we aim to enhance our services and facilities, ultimately transforming Siha Polyclinic Limited into a trusted and respected hospital and a center of excellence in healthcare.
